## Agenda

### 1. Project Committee Update

The Project Committee has held several meetings and has developed a well-fleshed-out Google Form with questions for prospective projects, including what applicants hope to gain from joining PRAGMA. PDR-3, the process document for prospective projects, has been updated to reflect the committee's decision to use a Google Form application process rather than requiring applicants to create a PDR directly. The application form is expected to be finalised by the end of February, with a target of opening applications to prospective projects in early March. A four-week application period has been proposed.

### 2. Maintainer Committee Update

The board noted that no recent updates have been received from the Maintainer Committee. Both Aiken and Amaru have outstanding quarterly reports that are overdue. The committee will be followed up with to obtain these reports.

### 3. Amaru Operations Document

Amaru has submitted an operations document describing how they operate as a project. The board has not yet reviewed the document. It was agreed that an email with a link to the document will be sent to board members so that they may review it and share feedback.

### 4. Credential and Asset Management Policy

The board discussed, at a high level, the question of which credentials and assets projects should hand over to PRAGMA so that the organisation holds administrator access to those resources. The board agreed that no decision is ready to be made at this time and that the discussion should be deferred. It was agreed that this topic will be included in the meeting minutes so that board members can review the current state of discussion ahead of next month's meeting, where a more focused conversation will be proposed.

### 5. Member Dues

The board reviewed a proposed annual member dues figure of CHF 8,900 per member organisation. The figure was presented as a reasonable estimate, with acknowledgment that adjustments may be needed depending on member uptake.

### 6. Legal Updates

No new legal updates were available since the last board interaction on this topic. Outstanding legal action items from the General Assembly remain open and are being tracked.

### 7. General Assembly – Open Coding Sessions Format Categories

An open action item from the General Assembly was noted: a request had been made for format categories relating to open coding sessions. The board agreed that a board member will reach out to the relevant contact to seek clarity on what was being requested, so that the board can assess whether and how to act on it ahead of the next meeting.

### 8. General Assembly – Virtual Annual Meeting

The board is required to hold an annual General Assembly meeting in April. The board discussed whether this meeting could be conducted virtually, noting that it is unlikely all members will be in the same location at that time. The board agreed to seek legal confirmation from counsel that a virtual meeting is permissible under PRAGMA's governing rules, and to proceed on that basis if confirmed.

### 9. PDR-3 Pull Request Review

The board was reminded that there is an open pull request containing updates to PDR-3. Board members were asked to review the pull request and to approve or comment on the proposed changes.

### 10. Next Meeting Date

The board discussed scheduling options for the next monthly meeting. The originally proposed date of Wednesday, 11 March 2026 was noted as problematic for some members due to travel commitments. The board agreed to schedule the next meeting for Wednesday, 18 March 2026 at the same time. A calendar invite and a link to the relevant document will be circulated.
